Instant noodles

Instant noodles are a quick and easy meal option that can be prepared in just a few minutes. Here's a simple recipe to make delicious instant noodles, This is my personal instant noodle recipe as i am a big instant noodle guy

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of instant noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • chilli flakes and seasoning that comes with the noodles
  • Optional toppings: boiled egg

Instructions

  1. boil 2 cups of water in a pot
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the instant noodles to the pot
  3. Cook the noodles for EXACTLY 2 minutes and 37 seconds
  4. When they are done drain around 2/3 of the water
  5. Add the seasoning and chilli flakes to the noodles and stir well to combine
  6. Serve the noodles in a bowl and add any optional toppings you like, such as a boiled egg

Enjoy your delicious and easy-to-make instant noodles!
