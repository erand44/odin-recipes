Trileqe

Trileqe is a traditional Albanian dessert that features layers of soft, moist sponge cake soaked in a rich, sweet milk mixture. The cake is drenched in a combination of condensed milk, evaporated milk, and regular milk, creating a luxurious, creamy texture. The dessert is then topped with a smooth caramelized layer, which adds a golden finish and enhances the sweetness. Trileqe is typically enjoyed chilled, allowing the flavors to meld perfectly. With its indulgent richness and delicate sweetness, Trileqe is a beloved treat for celebrations and special occasions in Albania. Its smooth texture and delightful flavor have made it a staple in Albanian pastry culture.

Trileqe is a light yet indulgent cake made with layers of sponge cake that absorb a blend of milks, resulting in a creamy and rich texture. Ingredients

  • Sponge Cake
  • Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Whole Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
